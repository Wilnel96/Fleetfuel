Why am I here?
If you're experiencing login issues (stuck on "Please wait..."), this page will help clear cached data that may be causing the problem.

Automatic Cache Clear

Manual Steps (if automatic didn't work)

  1. Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete (Windows) or Cmd + Shift + Delete (Mac)
  2. Select "All time" as the time range
  3. Check "Cached images and files"
  4. Click "Clear data"
  5. Close and reopen your browser
  6. Important: Do a hard refresh with Ctrl + F5 (Windows) or Cmd + Shift + R (Mac)
After clearing cache:
You'll need to log in again. The app should load the latest version and login should work normally.
